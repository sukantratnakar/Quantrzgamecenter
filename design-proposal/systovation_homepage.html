Our Approach

We don't do assembly-line publishing.

Most self-publishing services treat your book like a product to be manufactured — template covers, generic interiors, minimal editing, maximum volume. Your manuscript goes in one end and a book comes out the other, looking like every other self-published book on the shelf.

We work differently. Every book we publish begins with a conversation about what you're trying to accomplish — not just what you've written. Who needs to read this? What should they feel when they finish? What doors should this book open for you?

A book isn't a product. It's a permanent representation of your thinking. We treat it that way.

We take on a small number of projects at a time. Each one gets the attention it deserves — from structural editing to cover design to market positioning. The result is a book you'd be proud to hand to anyone, published under an imprint that stands for quality.

What We Offer

Five ways to get your book into the world

Every project is different. Some clients arrive with a complete manuscript. Others arrive with an idea and decades of experience but no written words. We meet you where you are.

01

Full Ghostwriting

You talk. We write.

You have the expertise but not the time — or the desire — to write a book yourself. Through deep extraction interviews, we capture your thinking, your voice, and your frameworks, then craft a complete manuscript that reads as if you wrote every word. Because intellectually, you did.

02

Guided Self-Publishing

You write. We guide and produce.

You want to write the book yourself, but you need structure, feedback, and a production partner who won't cut corners. We provide editorial guidance throughout the writing process, then handle everything from structural editing to cover design to distribution. Your words, our craft.

03

Imprint Publishing

Published by Systovation

Your manuscript is ready. You need a publisher who will treat it with respect — professional editing, premium cover design, interior layout, ISBN registration, and distribution across major platforms. Published under the Systovation imprint, your book carries the credibility of a publishing house, not a self-upload.

04

Manuscript Editing

Structural, developmental, or line editing

Your manuscript exists but it isn't ready. Maybe the structure wanders. Maybe the argument doesn't land. Maybe the prose needs tightening. We provide the editorial depth that turns a draft into a finished work — from big-picture structural editing to sentence-level polish.

05

Book Strategy & Positioning

Before you write a word

The most important decisions about your book happen before a single word is written. Who is this book for? What gap does it fill? How does it position you in your field? What's the one idea that makes this book worth someone's time? We work with you to answer these questions and produce a comprehensive book strategy — the blueprint that ensures every chapter, every page, every paragraph serves the larger purpose. Many of our clients start here before committing to a full project.

How It Works

From conversation to published book

Every project follows the same four phases, regardless of which service you choose. The depth and duration of each phase varies — a ghostwriting project takes 4-6 months, a manuscript edit might take 6 weeks — but the structure is consistent.

01

Discovery

We talk. Not about publishing — about what you've built, what you want to accomplish, and who you want to reach. This shapes everything that follows.

02

Architecture

We structure the book — outline, chapter flow, key arguments, narrative arc. For ghostwriting, this includes deep extraction interviews. For editing, this is the structural assessment.

03

Craft

Writing, editing, and refining. Every manuscript goes through multiple rounds — developmental, structural, and line editing. Cover design and interior layout happen in parallel.

04

Publication

ISBN, metadata, distribution, platform setup. Your book goes live on Amazon, IngramSpark, and wherever your readers are. Launch strategy and positioning support included.

Behind the Imprint

A publishing house built by someone who writes

Systovation Publishing was founded by Sukant Ratnakar — a Certified Management Consultant, author of over 30 books, and someone who has been on both sides of the publishing table. As an author published by Hay House and Amaryllis, and as a self-published writer, he's experienced everything from the quality of institutional publishing to the independence of doing it yourself.

Systovation exists to give professionals the best of both: the editorial depth, design quality, and legitimacy of a traditional publisher, with the speed, ownership, and creative control of self-publishing.

I built this imprint because I watched too many good books get ruined by bad production — and too many experienced people give up on publishing because the process felt overwhelming.

Every project is personally overseen. The research methodology that powers our ghostwriting is augmented by advanced tools that allow us to deliver at a speed and depth that traditional publishers can't match. But the craft — the editing, the structure, the design sensibility — is irreducibly human.
